For patients with myoclonus-dystonia

We want to create a global registry of patients with myoclonus-dystonia (MD) so that we can identify those who would be willing to be approached about taking part in future research projects on MD. It will also allow us to have a better idea of how many people have MD within the United Kingdom and eventually globally.
